# Visual Polish Deferred Items

These items should wait for approved BTI assets or business content. They would improve visual polish and perceived trust, but should not be fabricated or replaced with generic stock imagery.

| Page | Required asset or content | Why it would improve the page | Recommended format or dimensions | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Homepage | Approved hero photography or richer institutional imagery | A real BTI training or admissions image would make the first impression more credible and less abstract. | Landscape JPG/WebP, 1800x1200 minimum, with consent and licensing confirmed. | High |
| Homepage, About, Contact | Approved institute location or reception/training-room photos | Real location imagery would make BTI feel easier to trust before a visitor enquires or visits. | JPG/WebP, 1600x1000 minimum; include alt text describing the actual space. | High |
| Course catalogue and course detail pages | Course-specific imagery for English, IELTS, business, accounting, HR, law, technical, and youth programmes | Subject-specific visuals would help categories feel distinct instead of relying only on repeated text cards. | 4:3 or 16:10 JPG/WebP, 1200px wide minimum; avoid staged claims and ensure consent. | High |
| IELTS detail page and IELTS resource card | Approved IELTS-specific visual cues | Carefully approved IELTS visual material would make the page more immediately recognizable without implying unapproved outcomes or partnerships. | Image or icon set with reviewed usage rights; avoid partner logos unless approved. | Medium |
| Corporate Training | Approved workplace/team training imagery | A real corporate-training visual would support the stronger premium tone already present on the page. | Landscape JPG/WebP, 1600x1000 minimum; consent and licensing required. | Medium |
| Resources | Resource cover thumbnails | Covers would make guide cards feel more tangible and premium without pretending unavailable downloads exist. | PNG/JPG cover artwork, 800x1100, with clear title and BTI branding. | Medium |
| Site-wide proof sections | Institute-specific proof points | Approved proof points would strengthen trust and conversion, but numbers, accreditations, testimonials, and partner claims must be verified first. | Short approved statements with source/date; optional logo files only after legal approval. | High |
| Footer and contact areas | Final approved contact/location wording | Fully approved NAP, directions, hours, and visit notes would make conversion areas more concrete. | Text approved by BTI management; map URL if published. | High |
| Course detail pages | Approved schedules, fees, outcomes, and certificate wording | More specific information would reduce uncertainty and improve conversion quality. | Structured course-data updates with approval status tracked. | High |
| Legal and Arabic content | Professionally reviewed public copy | Complete reviewed legal and Arabic content would support public launch readiness. | Reviewed Markdown/structured content; Arabic copy must be native-reviewed. | Medium |
