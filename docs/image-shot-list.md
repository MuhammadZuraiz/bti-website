# Image Shot List

## Required Images

- `public/images/hero-training.jpg`: adult learner, admissions guidance or professional classroom scene. Recommended 16:10 or 4:3 crop, 1600x1100 minimum.
- `public/images/english-learning.jpg`: English-language learning scene. Recommended 4:3 crop, 1200x900 minimum.
- `public/images/ielts-preparation.jpg`: IELTS preparation materials or classroom. Recommended 4:3 crop, 1200x900 minimum.
- `public/images/corporate-training.jpg`: professional team training. Recommended 16:10 crop, 1400x900 minimum.
- `public/images/accounting-training.jpg`: professional accounting or finance learning context. Recommended 4:3 crop, 1200x900 minimum.
- `public/images/youth-programmes.jpg`: youth learning or computer-skills context. Recommended 4:3 crop, 1200x900 minimum.

## Fallback Strategy

The website uses branded fallback panels when optional images are missing. These fallbacks must not be described as photography or actual BTI learners.

## Guidance

- Use licensed or BTI-owned photography only.
- Do not present stock or generated people as actual BTI students.
- Get written consent for identifiable learners, minors, staff and trainers.
- Keep alt text descriptive and factual.
- Avoid fake success, guarantee or accreditation signals in images.
- Do not use identifiable minors without guardian consent.
- Record licence source, consent status and intended page placement for every final image.
