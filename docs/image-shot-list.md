# Image Shot List

## Required Images

- `public/images/hero-training.jpg`: adult learner or admissions guidance scene, 1600x1100 minimum.
- `public/images/english-learning.jpg`: English-language learning scene, 1200x900 minimum.
- `public/images/ielts-preparation.jpg`: IELTS preparation materials or classroom, 1200x900 minimum.
- `public/images/corporate-training.jpg`: professional team training, 1400x900 minimum.
- `public/images/accounting-training.jpg`: professional accounting or finance learning context, 1200x900 minimum.
- `public/images/youth-programmes.jpg`: youth learning or computer-skills context, 1200x900 minimum.

## Guidance

- Use licensed or BTI-owned photography only.
- Do not present stock or generated people as actual BTI students.
- Get written consent for identifiable learners, minors, staff and trainers.
- Keep alt text descriptive and factual.
- Avoid fake success, guarantee or accreditation signals in images.
